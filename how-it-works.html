Frequently Asked Questions

How do you determine your offer price?

We analyze recent comparable sales, current market conditions, property condition, and repair costs to provide a fair cash offer.

Are there any fees or commissions?

No. We don't charge any fees, commissions, or closing costs. The offer we make is what you receive.

How fast can you close?

We can close as fast as 7 days if needed, or we can wait up to 60 days to accommodate your schedule.

What if my house needs repairs?

We buy houses in any condition. You don't need to make any repairs or improvements before selling to us.
